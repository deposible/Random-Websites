📍 Where to find this information (official & free sources)

Currency rates: free, keyless currency-api (fawazahmed0/exchange-api) — every world fiat currency, daily, history from ~March 2024 (so currency timeframes are 1D–1Y). Bitcoin all-time price (House panel): Blockchain.com charts API (blockchain.com/charts), daily back to 2009. Each point is one day (not intraday ticks).
  • 🇺🇸 Federal Reserve — H.10 Foreign Exchange Rates: federalreserve.gov/releases/h10
  • 📊 FRED — Broad U.S. Dollar Index (DTWEXBGS): fred.stlouisfed.org/series/DTWEXBGS  — the dashed ＄ USD Index line is a DXY-style basket computed from live rates; FRED/ICE are the official references.
  • 🇪🇺 European Central Bank — Euro reference rates: ecb.europa.eu
  • 🇯🇵 Bank of Japan — daily FX rates: boj.or.jp
  • ₿ Blockchain.com — Bitcoin market price: blockchain.com/charts
  • ⏱️ Live intraday (paid/market): TradingEconomics · Investing.com
